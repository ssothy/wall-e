Wall-e's Friends

Aiko grew up on the streets of Baltimore, she got pregnant and had babies. Luckily her and her babies were taken to the shelter, but sadly all of her babies were adopted before her. Luckily Aiko was adopted by Wall-e's mom's son. Her new owner adores her and gives her cute nicknames like monkey and meeps. Aiko and her owner now live in The Netherlands so Aiko has came so far from her street life where she'd have to fight to survive. She even has part of her ear missing to prove it. It took her a while to learn to fully trust but her and her owner have came a long way. You can follow Aiko's new luxurious international life on instagram @aiko_zeeps.

Cupid is a 2 year old Bichon. His owner bought him when he was a baby. Cupid is also Wall-e's best friend, people might say they aint never seen two pretty best friends but they haven't met Wall-e and Cupid obviously. Cupid has so much energy and he's always so happy. After a Cupid and Wall-e playdate Wall-e usually snores when he sleeps because he'll be so tired.

Lizzy has been alive for 16 whole years and she still looks good for her age. Her dad is a cop so she has had a lot of training and can do almost any command even the one where she plays dead when you say bang. She's super nice but also super sassy. She probably won't give you the time of day if she knows you won't feed her.Fun fact is that she's named after Lizzie McGuire and her middle name is actually McGuire.

Cody is a Pomeranian-Collie mix so that basically means he's just a tall Pomeranian. He's so cute and energetic and can be described by his family as being pretty sensitive sometimes if you don't play with him which honestly I relate with. His human brothers are both wrestlers so Cody likes to watch them practice in the basement and they even sometimes practice moves with him.
